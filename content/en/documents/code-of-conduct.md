# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Queer Kit is dedicated to providing a safe, inclusive, and welcoming environment for all members of the LGBTQ+ community and allies.

## Expected Behavior

### Be Respectful

- Use inclusive and respectful language
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences
- Be mindful of how your words may affect others

### Be Supportive

- Offer encouragement and support to fellow community members
- Share knowledge and resources generously
- Help create a positive environment for everyone

### Be Inclusive

- Welcome people of all identities and backgrounds
- Use correct pronouns and names
- Avoid making assumptions about others

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated in our community:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sexual harassment or inappropriate advances
- Threats or intimidation

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to us at legal@queerkit.com. All reports will be handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- A warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban from the community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Queer Kit platforms, including our website, forums, social media channels, and community events.

## Acknowledgments

This Code of Conduct is adapted from best practices in inclusive community building.

## Contact

For questions about this Code of Conduct, contact us at legal@queerkit.com.
